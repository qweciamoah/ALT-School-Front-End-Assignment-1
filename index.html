Biography

Paul Amoah is a dedicated Physician Assistant with a strong background in patient care and clinical management. He began his medical journey at Central University, where he pursued a Bachelor of Science in Physician Assistantship from 2012 to 2016. During his studies, he developed a deep understanding of medical diagnostics, treatment protocols, and patient-centered care. After graduating, Paul gained hands-on experience in both primary and emergency care settings, honing his skills in diagnosing illnesses, prescribing treatments, and assisting in medical procedures. His commitment to healthcare and passion for improving patient outcomes have been evident throughout his career. Currently, Paul works at Hawa Memorial Saviour Hospital, where he plays a crucial role in providing medical care, managing patient cases, and collaborating with healthcare teams to ensure the best possible treatment plans. Beyond his clinical work, Paul has a keen interest in digital health, data analytics, exploring ways to integrate technology into healthcare for improved efficiency and patient care. His diverse skill set and continuous learning mindset make him a valuable asset to both the medical and tech industries. His passion for problem-solving and systems design led him to pursue cloud engineering to the professional level through AltSchool's structured program.
